Candidate Categories

The register should eventually cover many kinds of non-profits, because failure modes differ by category.

Human services

Food, housing, crisis support, disability services, family support, and direct care organizations.

Health and research

Medical charities, patient groups, research foundations, hospitals, and disease-specific organizations.

Education and culture

Schools, museums, cultural trusts, libraries, scholarship funds, and public education bodies.

Advocacy and civic life

Rights groups, policy institutes, civic infrastructure projects, watchdogs, and movement organizations.
